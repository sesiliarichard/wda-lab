01 — Annotation

High-Fidelity Annotation

We go beyond simple identification, capturing the nuances of species behavior and individual traits across terrestrial and aerial imagery.

Using specialized annotation software, our team produces structured, verified data that your research pipeline can immediately trust and build upon.

Species identification & behavioral tagging
Individual trait annotation (age, sex, condition)
Terrestrial & aerial image processing
Camera trap data annotation
Custom annotation schema development
02

02 — Data Management

Systematic Data Entry & Management

Even the most sophisticated analysis is only as good as the database it sits on. We handle the heavy lifting of systematic data entry, transforming raw field notes and sensor metadata into standardized, error-free formats.

Whether you use specialized conservation platforms or custom databases, we ensure every record is accurately entered, tagged, and ready for immediate reporting.

Field note digitization & standardization
Sensor & GPS metadata processing
Database entry across platforms (SMART, Airtable, Excel)
Error checking & validation protocols
Custom tagging and classification systems

03 — Analysis

Evidence-Based Data Analysis

We transform filtered datasets into high-level ecological insights. By applying rigorous statistical modeling, we move beyond simple observations to identify true population trends, habitat preferences, and behavioral patterns.

Our analysis provides the quantitative evidence required for reporting, decision making, and peer-reviewed research.

Population trend analysis & modeling
Habitat use & preference mapping
Behavioral pattern identification
Statistical reporting for publication
Data visualization & infographic output

No matter where your fieldwork takes you, we're the team behind the data.
